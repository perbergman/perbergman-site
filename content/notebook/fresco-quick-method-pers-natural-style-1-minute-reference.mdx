### 1. Canvas

- Off-white / pale yellow
- Opacity ~70%

### 2. Main Brush

Live Watercolor → Wash Soft

- Flow: ~75
- Water: ~35
- Pressure on

→ Use this for one long continuous line.

### 3. Color Bursts

Live Watercolor → Detail

- Saturation high
- Small brush

→ Add 3--5 intensity points.

### 4. Smudge (Wind Method)

Smudge → Soft Round

- Flow: 10--20%

→ Light touches only, like air.

### 5. Stop Rule

When the line feels alive, do not refine.

Your art emerges, it's not engineered.
